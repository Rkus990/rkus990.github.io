Introduction

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, advised by Prof. Supratik Chakraborty.

Research Interests
My current research is focused on synthesizing systems from relational specifications that are accurately represented in logic. To this end, I incorporate studies in knowledge compilation and synthesis as a means to guarantee correct-by-construction systems that find ubiquitous applications across AI. This is motivated by need of central issues pervading current black-box AI systems that are applied in safety-critical settings such as need of explainability, safety guarantees, interpretability and provable correctness.
